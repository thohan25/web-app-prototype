Splorg Rating: Ψ
Description: In order to make the common household item known as a plumbus, you first have to cut the dinglebop. Next, you smooth out the dinglebop with a bunch of shleem, which eventually gets repurposed for later batches. You then must take the dinglebop and push it through the krumbo. Once it has crossed over to the other side of the krumbo, you must rub a fleeb against it to create fleeb juice. The next step, and this is crucial, is that a shlami shows up and rubs the dinglebop and then spits on it. Finally, the ploobus and krumbo are shaved away, and boom, you have a plumbus.
